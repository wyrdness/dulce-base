## Research Notes

### Source Reliability Tiers

**Tier 1 (Most Reliable)**:
- Greg Bishop's *Project Beta* (well-researched, documented)
- Official government documents (FOIA releases)
- Geological and satellite data

**Tier 2 (Moderate)**:
- Mainstream journalism on conspiracy theories
- Academic studies of UFO mythology
- Skeptical analyses

**Tier 3 (Low Reliability)**:
- Phil Schneider lectures (unverified)
- UFO magazine articles (often uncritical)
- Conspiracy theory books

**Tier 4 (Unreliable)**:
- Thomas Castello materials (likely fabricated)
- Anonymous internet posts
- Unverified "leaked" documents

### Critical Evaluation

When researching Dulce Base claims:
- ✅ Verify sources and credentials
- ✅ Look for corroboration
- ✅ Check for documentation
- ✅ Consider psychological factors
- ❌ Don't accept testimony alone
- ❌ Don't assume lack of evidence = cover-up
